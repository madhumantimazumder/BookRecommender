FAQs

How should I get recommendation based on Genre?

If you simply want a quick recommendation based on some genere, click on 'find recommendation' button or go to https://www.bookaholics.ml/recommendation . In the footer you can find some genres. Click on any genre and you will get some recommendations.

How should I get recommendation based on my Goodreads profile?

If you donot have a goodreads profile then go to https://www.goodreads.com/ and setup your account with username. In our home page click on 'more recommendation?' button'. Click on 'Want recommendations based on my Goodreads profile ?' . Input your user id and click submit. You will get some recommendations.

Where can I find my user name?

You can find your user name by navigating to your account seetings and profile. Setup account with a username.

Why am I getting authorization error?

Update your 'who can view my profile' to anyone if you want to use our bookrecommendation system using goodreads profile. You can find your privacy settings by navigating ti your account setting and then settings.

How should I get recommendation based on my choice of book?

In our home page click on 'more recommendation?' button'. Click on 'Want recommendations based on a book you like?' . Search your favourite book and enter. You will get some recommendations.
